----> Social Media Checklist for a College Student

# Things You Should Do

- You should think carefully before you post anything on Social Media.

- Keep your Social Media profile clean and respectful at all times.

- When you are on Social Media try to share positive content.

- You should use the privacy settings on Social Media properly.

- Remember to stay polite when you are commenting or messaging on Social Media.

# Things You Should Not Do

- You should not share your details like your phone number or OTP on Social Media.

- Do not post anything that's offensive or inappropriate on Social Media.

- When you see news on Social Media do not believe it. Forward it if it is fake.

- Try not to compare your life with the lives of people on Social Media.

- You should not click on links or suspicious messages, on Social Media.

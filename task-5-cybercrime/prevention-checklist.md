# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

- Do not scan QR codes you do not know.
- Log out from devices you share with others.
- Never share your OTP or UPI PIN with anyone.
- Enable 2-factor authentication on accounts.
- Do not download apps from unknown websites.
- Keep passwords strong and different for each account.
- Do not click unknown links or attachments.
- Avoid public Wi-Fi for important logins

# In Case of Fraud

- Report at National Cyber Crime Portal (cybercrime.gov.in)
- Call 1930 immediately
